Shades of Blue

Do you see that girl from afar? The one with the crooked smile and confident gait? Clearly, she’s happy in one way or another. Perhaps she enjoys being outdoors or she’s on her way to yoga class or just had a great lunch; happy, but she could be so much more. Look closer. If you got close enough, could you see that she could feel herself breaking down, bit by bit, piece by piece?
